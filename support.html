Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q: How do I add a subscription?
    A: Tap "Add Subscription" and fill out the details like name, price, billing cycle, and reminder settings.
  • Q: Is my data stored securely?
    A: Yes — all your data is stored locally on your device and never shared or uploaded.
  • Q: How do reminders work?
    A: You can choose daily or one-time reminders before your renewal date, and even import them into your calendar.
